Youth STEAM Initiative Senior & Alumni Panel

Tuesday, August 11, 2026 | 6:00-8:00 PM EST | Zoom

The Youth STEAM Initiative will host a panel on Tuesday, August 11, 2026, from 6:00 PM to 8:00 PM EST. Recent high school graduates and Youth STEAM Initiative alumni will share advice about college applications, college life, academic exploration, and different paths after high school. Learn what they wish they had known, the lessons they've picked up along the way, and the advice they'd give to current students.

All students and families are encouraged to attend! Whether you're interested in learning more about high school or college, STEM, humanities, entrepreneurship, or simply exploring future opportunities, this panel offers advice from students who have recently been in your shoes.

The event will conclude with a live Q&A session where attendees may ask questions directly to the panelists.
